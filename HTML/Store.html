Information Request

Lamborghini travel items

When you opt for bags and backpacks proudly sporting the Lamborghini colors and style, you’ll take the charm and personality of Automobili Lamborghini with you wherever you go. You’ll enjoy the flawless look and practical aspects of our unique accessories, which we manufacture with the same care and attention to detail as we give to our exceptional automobiles. Lamborghini style will go everywhere you go.

Fashion for men and women

Transform yourself into a race car driver, experience thrills and excitement, and demonstrate your determination by wearing the Lamborghini colors and style, courtesy of our Squadra Corse menswear collection. We’ve designed our line of casual wear to emulate the unique character of the Lamborghini brand.
Our Squadra Corse Collection also offers a full line of women’s apparel, featuring the Automobili Lamborghini look and style. Manufactured in accordance with the strictest quality standards, utilizing the most advanced technologies, Lamborghini clothing is a veritable reflection of your personality.

Kids clothing

At Automobili Lamborghini, we’ve developed a full line of kids apparel, specially designed to keep up with the fast-paced, action-filled rhythm of today’s children. Our sweatshirts, T-shirts and jackets, clearly exuding the Lamborghini style, will enable your young adventurers to display their colors, with clothing that’s comfortable, practical and durable.

Lamborghini racing line

For seasoned and novice race car drivers, alike, Lamborghini offers a full range of clothing and accessories destined for the racetrack. From our Pilota shoes, precisely transmitting the sensations of the raceway, to our flame-retardant kart racing gear, enthusiasts will find just what they need to awaken the champion within. Topping off our catalogue are our Squadra Corse and OMP lines of undergarments and specialty wear.

Lego

Assembling LEGO® blocks is a pleasure for people of all ages. Just like the appreciation of a Lamborghini. It was in this spirit that the partnership between LEGO® and Automobili Lamborghini was born in order to create two small-scale models: the LEGO® Speed Champions Lamborghini Countach, designed for children and teens, and the Technic Lamborghini Sián FKP 37 (at a scale of 1:8), a challenge reserved for the most experienced enthusiasts of every age.
